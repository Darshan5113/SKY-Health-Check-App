Voting Guidelines

Understand how to cast your votes for team health checks

Green Vote

When to select: Team is performing strongly in this area.

  • Everything functioning as expected
  • No blockers, issues, or risks
  • Team confident in processes
  • Work on track, stakeholders informed
Amber Vote

When to select: Some concerns or areas for improvement.

  • Minor issues or blockers present
  • Support or corrective action needed
  • Team aware and working on improvements
  • Warning sign, not failure
Red Vote

When to select: Critical situation requiring immediate attention.

  • Serious challenges or stalled progress
  • Major blockers or unresolved issues
  • Failure in communication/execution
  • Urgent support or intervention needed
